About Us


Bringing Joy Since 2025

La Glace was founded in Miami, Florida by Mikayla Harris. She started this company as a way to give back to her community and build a space where people can socialize and feel at peace. La Glace is dedicated to providing a space for people to stop and enjoy life while embracing a sweet treat. To this day our mission is to spread joy to each and every customer that comes through our doors. We hope the next one will be you!
